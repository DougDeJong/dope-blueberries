Why Blueberry?

The berries are called 'superfood' for their amazing taste and extraordinary nutritious properties. Scientific research has shown that blueberries are amongst the most nutrient-dense berries, and are very low in calories! An entire cup contains 84 calories and 15 carbonhydrates, but have very high levels of vitamins, fibres, and antioxidants.
